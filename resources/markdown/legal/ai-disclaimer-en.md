# AI Disclaimer

## 1. Nature of AI Features

eFirm incorporates artificial intelligence ("AI") features powered by large language models to assist with legal document drafting, review, translation, and clause analysis. These features are tools to assist qualified legal professionals, not replacements for professional legal judgment.

## 2. No Legal Advice

AI-generated content does not constitute legal advice. All AI outputs must be reviewed, verified, and approved by a qualified lawyer before use in any legal proceeding, contract, or client communication.

## 3. Accuracy Limitations

While eFirm's AI is trained on legal vocabulary and conventions relevant to Jordanian law and Levant legal practice, AI outputs may contain:
- Inaccuracies or errors in legal reasoning
- Outdated references to laws or regulations
- Misinterpretation of context or intent
- Hallucinated citations or provisions

## 4. Professional Responsibility

The responsibility for the accuracy and appropriateness of any legal document remains with the licensed lawyer who reviews and approves it. Use of AI features does not diminish the lawyer's professional obligations under the Jordanian Lawyers Act or any applicable bar association rules.

## 5. Data Handling

AI prompts and responses are processed by third-party AI providers. eFirm does not allow AI providers to retain or train on your data. All AI interactions are logged in eFirm's audit system for accountability purposes.

## 6. Continuous Improvement

eFirm continuously works to improve AI accuracy and relevance for Arabic legal content. If you encounter inaccurate AI outputs, please report them to support@efirm.io.

## 7. Contact

For questions about AI features, contact support@efirm.io.
