# Data Processing Addendum

## 1. Scope

This Data Processing Addendum ("DPA") forms part of the Terms of Service between eFirm and the Customer. It applies to all processing of personal data by eFirm on behalf of the Customer.

## 2. Definitions

- **Personal Data:** Any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person.
- **Processing:** Any operation performed on personal data, including collection, storage, use, and deletion.
- **Data Controller:** The Customer, who determines the purposes and means of processing.
- **Data Processor:** eFirm, which processes personal data on behalf of the Customer.

## 3. Processing Instructions

eFirm shall process personal data only in accordance with the Customer's documented instructions and as necessary to provide the Service.

## 4. Security Measures

eFirm implements appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data, including:
- Encryption at rest and in transit (TLS 1.2+)
- Access controls and authentication
- Regular security assessments
- Audit logging of all data access

## 5. Sub-Processors

eFirm uses the following sub-processors:
- **DigitalOcean** (Frankfurt, DE) — Infrastructure hosting
- **Cloudways** (Frankfurt, DE) — Managed hosting
- **Stripe** (USA, EU) — Payment processing
- **Anthropic** (USA) — AI processing (no data retention by provider)

## 6. Data Breach Notification

eFirm shall notify the Customer of any personal data breach without undue delay and in any event within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach.

## 7. Data Deletion

Upon termination of the Service, eFirm shall delete all personal data within 30 days, unless retention is required by applicable law.

## 8. Audit Rights

The Customer has the right to audit eFirm's compliance with this DPA, subject to reasonable notice and confidentiality obligations.

## 9. Contact

For DPA inquiries, contact legal@efirm.io.
