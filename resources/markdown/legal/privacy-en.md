# Privacy Policy

## 1. Introduction

eFirm ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data in accordance with Jordan's Personal Data Protection Law No. 24/2023 (PDPL).

## 2. Data Controller

eFirm, based in Amman, Jordan, is the data controller for the personal data processed through the Service.

## 3. Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal data:
- **Account data:** Name, email address, password hash, profile information
- **Workspace data:** Firm name, matter details, document content, contact records
- **Usage data:** Log files, access times, features used, IP addresses
- **Payment data:** Processed by Stripe; we do not store full card numbers

## 4. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:
- Provide and maintain the Service
- Process payments and manage subscriptions
- Send service-related communications
- Improve the Service through aggregated analytics
- Comply with legal obligations

## 5. Data Storage and Residency

All data is stored on DigitalOcean servers in Frankfurt, Germany (FRA1 region). Your data does not leave the European Union.

## 6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:
- **Stripe:** For payment processing
- **DigitalOcean/Cloudways:** For hosting infrastructure
- **AI providers:** For AI features (prompts are processed but not stored by the provider)

## 7. Your Rights Under PDPL

You have the right to: access your data, correct inaccuracies, request deletion, restrict processing, and data portability. Contact legal@efirm.io to exercise these rights.

## 8. Cookies

See our cookie consent banner for details on cookie usage. Essential cookies are required for the Service to function.

## 9. Contact

For privacy inquiries, contact legal@efirm.io.
